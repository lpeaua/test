    About ATA Consulting

    The Pacific's Premiere IT Services Firm

    Company Overview

    Since 1993, ATA Consulting has been a trusted IT partner to clients not only statewide, but also in the national and international sectors. We currently have offices in Reno, Carson City, and Las Vegas and will continue to expand as we partner with more businesses that seek to work with a reputable Managed Service Provider (MSP).

    ATA Consulting’s structured approach allows organizations to better leverage their information technology and human resources so they can achieve greater levels of productivity, communication, security, and profitability. Our experienced consultants, engineers, and support staff use their management expertise to offer professional consulting and cost-effective IT management services to businesses.

    Our Vision

    ATA Consulting works with businesses of all sizes to increase productivity and profitability through the intelligent application and management of information technology solutions.
